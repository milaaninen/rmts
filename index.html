About

1. Company Policy



The Royal Metro Transit Services (from hiertho reffered to as "RMTS") is a company comissioned to run a metro service through the 2024 graduation show of the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.[1]



The leading idea behind our company is to create the perfect experience for the passenger.

Our stations will be at your service during the whole graduation show, which provides an opportunity and fast accessibility to shorten the time of travel inbetween several of the departments of the academy.

2. Our services



Our company's focus is sustaining permanent metro stations through the time of the graduation show. Our services will be freely accessible and constantly running through the opening times.
